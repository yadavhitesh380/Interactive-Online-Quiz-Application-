  <h1 class="center">📝 Online Quiz App</h1>

  <p class="center">
    An interactive <strong>quiz application</strong> built with <strong>HTML</strong>, <strong>CSS</strong>, and <strong>JavaScript</strong>.<br>
    Test your knowledge across various categories and difficulties, track your progress, and review your answers!
  </p>

  <div class="center badges">

  <h2>✨ Features</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>🎯 Choose category, difficulty, and number of questions</li>
    <li>⏳ Progress bar and timer for each question</li>
    <li>✅ Instant feedback on answers</li>
    <li>📊 Review incorrect answers at the end</li>
    <li>📱 Responsive and visually appealing UI</li>
  </ul>

  <hr>

  <h2>🚀 Try It Out</h2>
  <ol>
    <li>Download or clone this repository.</li>
    <li>Open <code>index.html</code> in your web browser.</li>
    <li>Select your quiz options and click <b>Start Quiz</b>!</li>
  </ol>

  <hr>

  <h2>💡 Example Categories & Questions</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>General Knowledge: “What is the capital of France?”</li>
    <li>Science: Computers: “What does CPU stand for?”</li>
    <li>History: “Who was the first President of the United States?”</li>
    <li>Entertainment: Music: “Who sang ‘Shape of You’?”</li>
  </ul>
